# Export Readiness Scoring Model

> [!IMPORTANT]
> **Prototype System Notice**  
> Caesar AI Scan is an offline static-analysis prototype. It identifies governance review needs, not final legal conclusions. The findings generated are AI usage signatures, not definitive proof of non-compliance. All evidence export candidates generated by the exporter remain in a `draft` status and require human review and cryptographic authorization before any official Caesar AI Evidence or Governance OS ingestion occurs.

This document describes the scoring logic, severity weights, and structural guardrails used by Caesar AI Scan to calculate finding readiness.

## 📊 Scoring Methodology

The export readiness score represents a numeric percentage from `0` to `100%` describing how complete the required compliance evidence is for a codebase component.

The score starts at a baseline of `100` and is decremented by severity-weighted deductions for each unresolved evidence gap:

$$\text{Export Readiness} = \max\left(0, 100 - \sum \text{Deductions}\right)$$

### 1. Gap Severity Weights

Deduction weights are based on the risk level associated with the missing evidence:

- **Critical & High Severity Gaps**: `-25%` per gap.
  - *Examples:* Missing model identification, missing vendor SLA, plain-text environment keys, missing privacy DPIA.
- **Medium Severity Gaps**: `-15%` per gap.
  - *Examples:* Missing system owner, missing processing purpose, missing cryptographic export signature.
- **Low Severity Gaps**: `-5%` per gap.
  - *Examples:* Missing high-level use-case summary, missing user-facing generative disclosure assessment.

---

## 🔒 Safety Guardrails

To prevent high-risk components from appearing "safe" under simplified scoring, Caesar AI Scan applies a strict **Blocking Cap**:

> [!CAUTION]
> **The 70% Blocking Cap**  
> If a finding contains even one outstanding gap with `blocks_export: true` (e.g. missing security review, missing vendor DPA), the calculated export readiness score is **capped at a maximum of 70%**, regardless of other completed metrics.

This ensures that any component missing critical security, privacy, or legal clearances is immediately flagged as blocked and cannot be treated as ready.

## 🛠️ Offline Mock Examples

To see the scoring model in action, consider these four canonical types of signals scanned in the sample project:

### 1. AI API Credentials (`env-var-detector`)
- **Outstanding Gaps**: `missing_system_owner` (Med, blocks), `missing_security_review` (High, blocks), `missing_model_or_provider_identification` (High, blocks), `missing_export_authorization` (Med, blocks).
- **Calculated Score**: $100 - 15 - 25 - 25 - 15 = 20\%$
- **Export Status**: `blocked_missing_context`

### 2. AI SDK Dependency (`dependency-detector`)
- **Outstanding Gaps**: `missing_system_owner` (Med, blocks), `missing_processing_purpose` (Med, blocks), `missing_ai_use_case_description` (Low), `missing_model_or_provider_identification` (High, blocks), `missing_vendor_terms_review` (High, blocks), `missing_risk_classification` (High, blocks), `missing_export_authorization` (Med, blocks).
- **Calculated Score**: $100 - 15 - 15 - 5 - 25 - 25 - 25 - 15 = 0\%$ (floored)
- **Export Status**: `blocked_missing_context`

### 3. Prompt Configuration File (`prompt-file-detector`)
- **Outstanding Gaps**: `missing_processing_purpose` (Med, blocks), `missing_human_oversight_description` (Med), `missing_logging_or_retention_policy` (Med), `missing_user_disclosure_assessment` (Low), `missing_export_authorization` (Med, blocks).
- **Calculated Score**: $100 - 15 - 15 - 15 - 5 - 15 = 35\%$
- **Export Status**: `blocked_missing_context`

### 4. Vector DB Pipeline (`vector-db-detector`)
- **Outstanding Gaps**: `missing_data_processing_assessment` (High, blocks), `missing_logging_or_retention_policy` (Med), `missing_processing_purpose` (Med, blocks), `missing_export_authorization` (Med, blocks).
- **Calculated Score**: $100 - 25 - 15 - 15 - 15 = 30\%$
- **Export Status**: `blocked_missing_context`
