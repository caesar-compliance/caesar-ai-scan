## ⚖️ Disclaimer & Policy Boundary

- **Offline Scanner Prototype:** `caesar-ai-scan` is a static-analysis utility. It does not guarantee regulatory or legal compliance. Findings are signals, not proof of non-compliance.
- **Human Oversight:** All evidence export candidates generated by this tool start as drafts and strictly require human review and sign-off before official ingestion into Governance OS. Status remains locked in `draft` with `review_required: true`.
- **Static Demo Sandbox Only**: The public deployed site represents an offline visual mockup utilizing synthetically generated mock assets. Zero network-active ingestion or dynamic customer codebase processing is initiated.
